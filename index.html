What worked

✅ Interest connection

When the interest was genuinely woven in, engagement noticeably increased

✅ Learning style detection

Users felt the quiz was accurate — it removed the stigma of self-labeling

What didn't

⚠️ Depth at lower grades

Early versions were too simple — needed more explicit grade-level calibration

⚠️ Loading times

AI generation takes time — required multiple iterations to optimize for speed
